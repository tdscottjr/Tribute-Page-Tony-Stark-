The Timeline of Tony Stark's life:

  • 2008 -Becomes Iron Man!
  • 2010 -Created a new element to enhance the arch reactor.
  • 2012 -The battle of New York.
  • 2013 -Iron Man takes on the terrorist the Mandarin.
  • 2015-The age of Ultron begins.
  • 2016 -The Avengers have an eternal war that will forever change the course of time.
  • 2017-Tony begins to mentor a new hero called Spiderman.
  • 2018-Thanos begins to attack the earth while the Avengers try to stop him.
  • 2023-After a long journey the Avengers defeated Thanos after Tony Stark heroically sacrificed his life.
